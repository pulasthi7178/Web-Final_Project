The central feature of Sri Lankan cuisine is boiled or steamed rice, served with a curry of fish or meat, along with other curries made with vegetables, lentils, or fruits. Dishes are accompanied by pickled fruits or vegetables, chutneys, and sambols.

About us

Sri Lankan food counts among some of the most satisfying and sumptuous cuisines. Sri Lanka‘s capital, Colombo, is increasingly becoming a hot spot for premium restaurants looking to give visitors a taste of the country’s flavors. From creamy curries to spicy rotties and the traditional string hoppers, we have eaten our way through Colombo’s best restaurants and put together this list. Take your pick from the best Sri Lankan food haunts in the multi-cultural city of Colombo.

In areas located on the island's coasts seafood is a standard feature of the local dishes. Tamil cuisine, especially in Jaffna, shares many similarities with South Indian cuisine. Kandyan Sinhalese cooking is based on the local ingredients of the hill vegetables and fruits.
